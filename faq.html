Answers to Common Questions

Everything you need to know about enrollment, tuition, and policies at Legacy Land Academy.

Our application fee is a one-time fee of $50 and must be paid at the time of application submission. This is required to hold your child's slot and guarantee that your child's slot is available.
We operate from 6:30 AM – 6:00 PM, Monday - Friday. After 6:00 PM, a $1.00 per minute late fee will be applied.
Tuition rates vary by age group and enrollment schedule. Please contact us directly for current rates — we'd love to walk you through your options.
Legacy Land Academy uses the Georgia Early Learning and Development Standards (GELDS) for children ages birth through five years. Our curriculum is designed to promote school readiness while nurturing creativity, confidence, and a love for learning.
At Legacy Land Academy, tuition is billed on enrollment and not attendance. We encourage regular attendance.
Tuition is due on a weekly basis. It is based on enrollment and not attendance. Weekly tuition is due at the start of each week on Monday at the time of check-in when your child is signed in for care.
Yes, we follow Bright From the Start's sick policy, which states that if a child has a fever of 101°F (38.3°C) or higher with other signs of illness, the child will not be allowed to be present at school. In order to return, the child must be fever-free for 24 hours without the use of medication, and showing no signs of illness. Also, if your child becomes sick during the day, they will be moved to a quiet, separated area and monitored until they are picked up. Children who become sick during the day must be picked up within 1 hour of the facility notifying the parent. If we cannot reach the parent(s), we will contact other authorized pick-up persons on the list.
We use the Playground software app for communication, daily updates, attendance, tuition payments, and more.
Families complete enrollment forms, provide immunization records, complete emergency medication administration forms, authorized pick-up forms, and sign the operational policy and parent handbook.
Enrollment is open to children ages 6 months through 5 years.
We only accept online payments (credit or debit card) via the Playground app. No other forms of payment will be accepted. This ensures you receive the most accurate end-of-year report.
