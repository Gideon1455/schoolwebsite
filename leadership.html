Meet Our Founder

Josephine Ogunwale

Founder & Director — "Ms. O"

"Thank you for trusting Legacy Land Academy with your most precious gift — your child."

B.Sc. Educational Management M.Ed. Early Childhood Education Early Childhood Education Certified Ed.S. Advanced Educational Studies Special Education Certified CPR & First Aid Certified Georgia DECAL Compliant

My name is Josephine Ogunwale, and I am honored to serve as the Director and Teacher at Legacy Land Academy. With more than 30 years of experience in education, I have devoted my career to helping children learn, grow, and thrive in a nurturing and supportive environment.

I hold a master's degree (M.Ed.) in early childhood education and an Education Specialist (Ed.S.) degree in Advanced Educational Studies, reflecting my commitment to providing high-quality care and educational excellence for every child entrusted to me.

As an educator, my Christian faith is the foundation of my approach to teaching and caring for children. I strive to create a warm, welcoming atmosphere where every child feels safe, loved, respected, and valued. I believe that early childhood education is more than academic growth — it is also about building confidence, character, and a strong sense of belonging. To ensure the safety and well-being of every child, I am CPR and First Aid Certified.

Working with children is my passion, and I am dedicated to fostering curiosity, creativity, and a lifelong love of learning. I look forward to partnering with families and supporting each child as they reach important developmental milestones and discover their unique potential.

Thank you for trusting Legacy Land Academy with your most precious gift — your child.
