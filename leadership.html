Meet Our Founder

Josephine Ogunwale

Founder & Director — "Ms. O"

"Every child who walks through our doors carries an extraordinary future. My life's calling is to see it, believe in it, and help them step boldly into it."

M.Ed. Early Childhood Education M.Ed. Educational Management Ed.S. Advanced Educational Studies Special Education Certified CPR & First Aid Certified Georgia DECAL Compliant

Ms. O founded Legacy Land Academy with a singular vision: to create a learning environment where every child is known by name, celebrated for who they are, and challenged to reach their full God-given potential.

With three advanced degrees in education and over three decades of hands-on experience in early childhood development, Ms. O brings a depth of knowledge and a warmth of spirit that sets Legacy Land apart. She is not just an administrator — she is a daily presence in classrooms, a mentor to staff, and a trusted partner to every family we serve.

Her commitment to excellence is reflected in the academy's full compliance with Georgia DECAL standards and its alignment with the Bright from the Start initiative — not because it is required, but because our children deserve nothing less.
